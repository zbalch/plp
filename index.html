Bell Options

Yellow Brass

Our standard bell option. Yellow brass achieves the brightest sound, brilliance, and projection.

Gold Brass

Gold brass bells offer a darker, fuller sound than yellow brass but still able to produce a brilliant sound with good projection.

Red Brass

Red brass bells offer a warmer, mellow sound. Does not project as well as gold or yellow brass.

* Yellow and gold brass bells are available in a lightweight configuration for faster response.
